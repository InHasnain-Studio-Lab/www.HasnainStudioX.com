Privacy Policy

WebX Studio App - How your data is handled and protected.

Effective date: 5 Jun 2026  ·  Version: 1.0.0.0  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

WebX Studio is a Windows desktop visual website builder. You design pages on a drag-and-drop canvas and export clean HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

WebX Studio performs all editing locally on your device and does not transmit your projects or personal data to Hasnain Studio X.

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

WebX Studio does not collect or send the following to the developer:

Identity Data

Name, email, phone number, or personal identity data. The app has no user accounts.

Your Projects

The websites, pages, code and images you create are never uploaded to our servers.

Telemetry

No analytics, ad tracking, or third-party telemetry SDKs are used.

Sensitive System Data

No password, credential, keychain, or hidden account data is collected.

3. Data Stored Locally

Projects & Assets

Your .wxs project files, imported images and design tokens are saved to locations you choose on your device.

App Settings

Preferences such as theme and start-with-Windows are stored locally.

Exports

Exported sites (.zip / HTML) are created only when you explicitly choose to export.

4. Network & Third-Party Services

WebX Studio makes no background network calls. Network access happens only when you explicitly act:

Publishing

Netlify, GitHub or FTP upload connects only to the destination you choose and sends only your exported site files. Credentials you enter are used for that session and are not stored by the app.

Imported Sites

If you import a page that links absolute web fonts or assets (e.g. Google Fonts), your browser engine loads them directly from those providers.

External Links

Opening a link you click launches your default browser. No personal data is attached.

5. Microsoft Store Purchases

WebX Studio is sold through the Microsoft Store with a free trial. Payment, trial management and licensing are handled by Microsoft Store under Microsoft policies.

WebX Studio does not process or store payment card details.

6. App Permissions

WebX Studio requests only internet client access (for the optional publishing features above) and runs as a full-trust desktop app so it can save files you choose. No system information is collected or transmitted.

7. Children's Privacy

WebX Studio is intended for general productivity and creative users and is not directed to children under 13.

8. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and version on this page.
