Privacy Policy

XSeasons App - How your data is handled and protected.

Effective date: 23 May 2026  ·  Version: 1.0.0.0  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

XSeasons is a Windows desktop companion that turns your desktop into a living, seasonal canvas of glassy widgets - weather, clocks, ambience, live wallpapers and more.

XSeasons runs locally on your device and does not transmit personal data to Hasnain Studio X.

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data. There are no analytics or advertising SDKs in the app.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

XSeasons does not collect or send the following to the developer:

Identity Data

No name, email, phone number, or account identity is collected by the app.

Telemetry

No analytics, crash tracking, ad tracking, or third-party telemetry SDKs are used.

Sensitive System Data

No passwords, credentials, keychain, files, or hidden account data are collected.

Precise Tracking

No continuous GPS tracking or movement history. Location is used only to look up weather (see Section 4).

3. Data Stored Locally

Settings & Layout

Your widget layout, theme, units and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Chosen City / Coordinates

If you set or detect a weather location, the city name and approximate coordinates are saved locally to fetch your forecast.

Your Media

Wallpapers, sounds and signature text you add stay on your device; nothing is uploaded.

License State

Trial and full-version status is managed by the Microsoft Store licensing service; XSeasons keeps no separate license file.

4. Network & Third-Party Services

XSeasons makes network requests only for the weather feature.

Open-Meteo weather. To show current conditions and the forecast, XSeasons sends your approximate location (latitude/longitude) or a city search term to the free, key-less Open-Meteo service (open-meteo.com). No account, name, or personal identifier is sent - only the coordinates or city needed to return weather.

Open-Meteo processes these requests under its own privacy policy. If you do not set a location, no weather request is made. XSeasons does not contact any other servers.

5. Microsoft Store Purchase & Trial

XSeasons is a paid app with a Microsoft Store-managed free trial. Purchase, trial eligibility, billing and licensing are handled entirely by the Microsoft Store under Microsoft's policies.

XSeasons does not process or store any payment card details, and does not implement its own purchase or licensing servers.

6. App Permissions

XSeasons requests only the minimum capabilities required to run: internet access (for weather) and standard desktop (full-trust) execution to render its overlay, set the wallpaper you choose, and read local system status (CPU, memory, battery and the current media "Now Playing" title) for its widgets. This information is shown on your screen and is never transmitted.

7. Children's Privacy

XSeasons is a general-audience desktop personalization app and is not directed to children under 13.

8. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and version on this page.
