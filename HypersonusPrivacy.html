Privacy Policy

Hypersonus Ultra - How your data is handled and protected.

Effective date: 19 June 2026  ·  Version: 1.0.0  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

Hypersonus Ultra is a Windows spatial audio enhancement app. It applies cinematic surround, spatial and Hyper 3D audio, pro presets, and a 10-band equaliser to every app on your PC using the WASAPI audio pipeline.

Hypersonus Ultra processes all audio entirely on your PC. Your audio stream is never captured, recorded, or transmitted anywhere outside your machine.

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Hypersonus Ultra does not collect or send the following to the developer:

Identity Data

No name, email, phone number, account, or sign-in is ever requested or stored.

Your Audio

Audio processed through Hypersonus Ultra stays entirely on your machine. Nothing is captured or sent.

Usage Patterns

The app does not track which features you use, how long you use them, or what presets you choose.

Telemetry

No analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, no third-party trackers of any kind are included.

3. Data Stored Locally

Audio Settings

Your selected preset, equaliser bands, surround mode, and spatial configuration are saved locally so your preferred sound profile is restored on every launch.

Device Profiles

Per-device latency, output mode, and calibration state are stored locally under your Windows user profile.

App Preferences

Theme, window layout, and UI preferences are saved in local application storage.

Licence State

A cached licence flag lets the app recognise your purchase offline, without contacting the Microsoft Store on every launch.

All local data is stored under %LOCALAPPDATA%\Packages\HasnainButtAkhtar.HypersonusUltra\. Uninstalling the app removes all of this data.

4. Network Activity

Microsoft Store Licence Check

On first launch and when you tap Restore Purchase, Hypersonus Ultra contacts the Microsoft Store API to verify your licence. Only your Store account token is used - the app never sees your payment details.

Outbound Links

Tapping hasnainstudiox.com or the privacy-policy link opens your default browser. No data is sent by the app itself in this action.

Beyond licence verification, Hypersonus Ultra makes no outbound network requests.

5. Microsoft Store Purchases

All purchases and the free trial are managed entirely by the Microsoft Store. Hypersonus Ultra never sees your payment card, billing address, or Microsoft account credentials.

The app receives only a licence object confirming whether your account holds an active purchase or an active trial, and the trial expiry date if applicable. No personally identifiable information is returned by this API call.

6. App Permissions

Internet Client

Required for Microsoft Store licence verification and to open web links in your browser.

Full Trust

Required to access the WASAPI audio pipeline for system-wide spatial audio enhancement. No elevated access is used beyond audio processing.

7. Children's Privacy

Hypersonus Ultra is a general-purpose audio enhancement tool and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and version number at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
