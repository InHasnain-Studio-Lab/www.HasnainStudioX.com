Privacy Policy

HSX Vanta App - How your data is handled and protected.

Effective date: 12 Jun 2026  ·  Version: 1.0.0.0  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

HSX Vanta is a Windows desktop app that generates images from text prompts using an AI engine that runs entirely on your own computer.

HSX Vanta performs all generation, face processing, and image enhancement locally on your device. Your prompts, face photos, and images are never uploaded and are never transmitted to Hasnain Studio X.

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

HSX Vanta does not collect or send the following to the developer:

Identity Data

Name, email, phone number, or personal identity data.

Prompts and Images

Your prompt text, face photos, and generated images are never uploaded. They exist only on your device.

Telemetry

No analytics, ad tracking, or third-party telemetry SDKs are used.

Sensitive System Data

No password, credential, keychain, or hidden account data is collected.

3. Data Stored Locally

Generated Images

Images you create are saved to your Pictures folder, with their prompt and settings kept in a local library index on your device.

Settings

Theme, accent, and engine preferences are stored locally in your user profile.

Licence State

Your trial or purchase state is managed by the Microsoft Store; the app reads it locally and stores nothing about your payment.

4. Content Safety and Reporting

HSX Vanta ships with always-on content protection: prompts requesting nudity or explicit content are blocked before generation, safety terms are applied to every generation, and any prompt combining minors with sexual content is refused outright. These protections cannot be disabled.

The Library includes a voluntary "report" action. It opens your own email client with a pre-filled message to the developer; nothing is sent automatically, and you choose what to include. Reports are used solely to improve the app's safety in future updates.

5. Microsoft Store Purchase and Trial

HSX Vanta is a paid app with a Microsoft Store managed free trial. Payment and transaction processing are handled by the Microsoft Store under Microsoft policies.

HSX Vanta does not process or store payment card details.

6. App Permissions

HSX Vanta runs as a full trust desktop app and declares internet access, which is used only for the Microsoft Store licence check and the links you choose to open. Image generation itself requires no network connection. Permissions are used only for app functionality and not for data harvesting.

7. Children's Privacy

HSX Vanta is intended for general creative users and is not directed to children under 13. Its content protections for minors are always enabled and cannot be turned off.

8. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and version on this page.
