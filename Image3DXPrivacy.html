Privacy Policy

Image3D X is developed by Hasnain Studio X with a local‑first, privacy‑first design philosophy. Your photographs are read, converted to a 3D relief, and exported entirely on your own PC.

Effective date: 10 July 2026  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar / Hasnain Studio X  ·  Applies to: Image3D X (Windows App)

1. Overview

Image3D X is developed by Hasnain Studio X with a local-first, privacy-first design philosophy.

  • We do not collect personal data.
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers.
  • We do not operate servers for our apps.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

All processing happens locally on your device. Depth is solved, the mesh is built, the surface is lit, and every file is written on your own PC.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Image3D X does not collect, store, or send any of the following:

Personal Identity Data

No name, email, phone number, address, or identifiers.

Usage Data or Analytics

No telemetry, crash analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Your Images and Projects

No photographs, depth maps, meshes, project files, or anything you create inside the app.

Sensitive System Data

No passwords, tokens, credentials, browsing history, or system logs.

Location Data

No GPS, IP-based location, or device location. Image metadata is never read for location.

We do not run servers, so nothing can be uploaded even by accident.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

App Settings

Your theme, accent, mesh density, viewport preference, and the list of recently opened files are saved locally on your device only.

Your Reliefs and Projects

Project files, exported meshes, and PNG snapshots are written where you choose to save them, and are never transmitted.

Working Cache

The solved depth map and shaded surface are held in memory while you work, and are rebuilt from your image each session.

Local Trial or Licence State

Managed entirely by the Microsoft Store. The app reads it locally; we receive nothing.

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

4. Microsoft Store Licensing

Image3D X is a paid app with a Microsoft Store managed free trial. All licensing, payments, and transactions are handled exclusively by Microsoft.

  • We do not process payment information.
  • We do not store payment details.
  • We do not receive your Microsoft account information.

The app asks Windows for its own licence status so it knows whether the trial is active. That check happens between your PC and Microsoft.

5. App Permissions

Minimum Required Only

Image3D X declares only internet client access and desktop full trust. Nothing else is requested, and no permission is used for data collection.

File Access

Only the images you open, drop, or choose, and only the files you choose to save. There is no background scanning of your disk.

On-Device Processing

Depth estimation, mesh generation, lighting, shadows, and colour grading all run on your CPU and graphics adapter. No image is ever uploaded.

Clipboard Access

Only when you paste an image into the app.

Network Access

Used only for the Microsoft Store licence check and for opening our website or the Store page when you click those links.

Hardware Reading

The app reads your processor name, core count, memory size, and graphics adapter name to show an honest performance expectation. These values stay on your PC.

6. Children's Privacy

Image3D X is a general-purpose creative utility. It is not directed to children under 13 and does not collect any data from any user.

7. Policy Updates

If this policy changes, we will update the version and effective date. Any changes will remain consistent with our core principle: Your data stays on your device.
