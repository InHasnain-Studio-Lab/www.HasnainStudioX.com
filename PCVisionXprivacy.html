PC VisionX Privacy Policy

PC VisionX is a fully local display comfort engine. It has no account, no sign-in, and no cloud.

Effective date: 26 June 2026 · Version 1.0.0.0 · Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar (Hasnain Studio X)

1. Overview

PC VisionX runs entirely on your PC. It does not create an account, does not require a sign-in, and does not send your usage, settings, or screen content to us or anyone else. We do not operate analytics or telemetry, and we never sell data because we never collect it.

2. Data we do not collect

  • No identity: no name, email, or account.
  • No telemetry, analytics, crash uploads, or usage tracking.
  • No screen captures, no camera or webcam access, no microphone access.
  • No advertising identifiers and no cross-app or cross-site tracking.

3. Data stored locally on your PC

Your preferences live in readable files under %LOCALAPPDATA%\PCVisionX, which you can inspect, export, import, or delete at any time from the Privacy page in the app:

  • settings.json - your display, circadian, awareness, accessibility, and privacy preferences, including coordinates you enter for the circadian schedule.
  • eyehealth.json - local screen-time and break counts, kept only to show your own insights.
  • theme.dat - the chosen appearance mode and accent.

None of these files leave your machine unless you choose to export and share them yourself.

4. Network and third-party services

PC VisionX uses the internet only to improve your experience, never to collect data. There are only two kinds of call, both of which simply return a value the app uses on your PC:

  • Location. A one-off request to a public IP-geolocation service (ip-api.com) returns approximate coordinates for your network so the circadian schedule knows day from night. The result is pinned locally and never looked up again until you clear it. As with any web request the service sees your IP address; no account or other personal data is sent. If you prefer, you can set your location offline by typing a country or city name, with no network call at all.
  • Weather-aware glare (off by default). When enabled, the app sends only the latitude and longitude you saved to Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) to read the current cloud cover, then discards the response.

No personal data is collected, nothing is stored remotely, and a network-activity indicator on the Privacy page lights only while one of these lookups is in progress.

5. Microsoft Store purchase and trial

PC VisionX is sold through the Microsoft Store with a Store-managed free trial. Purchases, billing, and the trial are handled by Microsoft under Microsoft's terms and privacy policy; PC VisionX never sees or stores your card details. The app only asks Windows whether the current install is licensed.

6. App permissions

The app declares internetClient (for the optional weather call only) and runFullTrust (required for a desktop app that adjusts your displays). It reads display, light-sensor, and battery information locally to do its job; this information is used on your PC and is never transmitted.

7. Children's privacy

PC VisionX is a general-audience utility and is not directed to children under 13. It collects no personal information from anyone.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the effective date and version above are updated. Any no-telemetry and local-only statements here will always remain literally true for the shipped app.
