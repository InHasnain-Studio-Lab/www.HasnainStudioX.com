Privacy Policy

ForgeX Pro App - How your data is handled and protected.

Effective date: 1 Aug 2026  ·  Version: 1.0.0.0  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

ForgeX Pro is a Windows desktop productivity app for prompt building and AI workflow organization.

ForgeX Pro performs processing locally on your device and does not transmit personal data to Hasnain Studio X.

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

ForgeX Pro does not collect or send the following to the developer:

Identity Data

Name, email, phone number, or personal identity data.

Prompt Content Upload

Your prompt text is not automatically uploaded to our servers.

Telemetry

No analytics, ad tracking, or third-party telemetry SDKs are used.

Sensitive System Data

No password, credential, keychain, or hidden account data is collected.

3. Data Stored Locally

Local Library

Saved prompts and app settings are stored locally on your device.

Trial/License State

A local license file may store trial start date and Pro activation state.

Exports

Exported files are created only when you explicitly choose to export.

4. Microsoft Store Purchases

ForgeX Pro may offer a Microsoft Store durable add-on for lifetime Pro access. Payment and transaction processing are handled by Microsoft Store under Microsoft policies.

ForgeX Pro does not process or store payment card details.

5. App Permissions

ForgeX Pro requests only the minimum permissions required to run core desktop features. Permissions are used only for app functionality and not for data harvesting.

6. Children's Privacy

ForgeX Pro is intended for general productivity users and is not directed to children under 13.

7. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and version on this page.
