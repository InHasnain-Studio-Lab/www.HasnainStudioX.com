3. Information We Collect

Mobile TuneX is designed to perform on-device optimisation and diagnostics. Most processing happens locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our servers.

A. Information processed on your device

These operations happen locally and are never sent to us:

Device Diagnostics

Battery status, storage usage, memory info, and network test results - processed entirely on-device.

Scan Results

Cleaner scan, app scan, and gallery scan results based on your selected actions - never uploaded.

Local Preferences

App settings and preferences such as theme, language, and feature configuration stored on your device only.

Permission Statuses

Granted or denied permission states used locally to enable or disable specific features.

B. Permissions requested (as needed)

Mobile TuneX may request the following permissions to enable features you choose:

Storage & Media

Photos, videos, audio, or storage access for cleaner and gallery features.

Notifications

Notification permission for in-app alerts and reminders.

Battery Optimisation

Battery and booster-related permissions required for power-saving features.

Network

Network permissions for speed tests, ads delivery, and connectivity checks.

C. Third-party SDK data

Mobile TuneX uses Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) for advertising. AdMob may collect:

Advertising Identifiers

Device advertising IDs used by Google AdMob for ad targeting and measurement.

Device Identifiers

Device-level identifiers used by AdMob for frequency capping and fraud prevention.

Approximate Location

IP-based approximate location used by AdMob for regional ad serving.

App Interaction & Diagnostics

App usage signals and diagnostic data collected by AdMob according to Google's own policies.

This data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policies and not by Mobile TuneX. Google AdMob is the only third-party service that may collect data from your device.

4. How We Use Information

App Features

To run cleaner, battery optimiser, game booster, scans, and reports.

Local Preferences

To save your local settings and app configuration across sessions.

Advertising

To display ads for non-premium users through Google AdMob.

App Improvement

To improve stability, performance, and overall user experience.

Legal Compliance

To comply with applicable legal obligations where required.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Local-Only Processing

Core feature data is processed and stored locally on your device only.

No Account Required

We do not require account registration. Your identity is never collected.

No File Uploads

We do not store your personal files or scan results on our servers.

Third-Party Retention

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may process data according to their own retention policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data only with:

  • Google AdMob - integrated for ads and related services (non-premium users only).
  • Authorities - only if required by applicable law.

7. International Transfers

Third-party providers such as Google may process data on servers located outside your country. These providers apply their own safeguards and compliance controls in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

8. Your Choices & Controls

Permissions

App Settings Reset

Reset all app settings and stored preferences from within the app.

Ad Personalisation

Limit ad personalisation through your Google or Android advertising settings.

Uninstall

Uninstall the app at any time to stop all on-device data processing.

10. Security
