Privacy Policy

FlipX Studio App - How your data is handled and protected.

Effective date: 30 May 2026  ·  Version: 0.1.1  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

FlipX Studio is an Android media-conversion app for video, audio, image, and document files.

FlipX Studio performs every conversion locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to Hasnain Studio X servers.

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

FlipX Studio does not collect or send the following to the developer:

Identity Data

No name, email, phone number, account, or sign-in is ever requested.

Your Media Files

Videos, audio, images, and documents you convert never leave your device.

Location & Contacts

The app never requests location data or reads your contacts.

Telemetry

No analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, no third-party trackers.

3. Data Stored Locally

Output Files

Conversions you run are written to FlipX Studio's app-specific storage on your device.

Job Queue & Library

A local SQLite database tracks job status, progress, and the catalogue of finished outputs.

Preferences

Theme variant, light / dark choice, reduced-effects flag, haptics toggle, and concurrency setting are saved with SharedPreferences.

Pro Entitlement Flag

A boolean cached locally so Pro features continue to work offline after purchase.

Uninstalling FlipX Studio removes all of this local data.

4. Network Activity

Purchase Validation

When you buy or restore Pro, the Play Billing token is sent over HTTPS to a Hasnain Studio X edge function which verifies the purchase with Google Play. Only the product id, purchase token, and package name are sent.

Google Fonts

The Inter and Cormorant Garamond fonts are fetched on first launch and cached on disk.

Google AdMob (Free tier)

When ads are enabled, AdMob loads native ad templates. Your media files are never shared with AdMob.

Outbound Links

Tapping hasnainstudiox.com or the support email opens an external browser or mail app.

5. Advertising (Free Tier)

If you are on the Free tier and ads are enabled in your build, FlipX Studio shows native ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your Android Advertising ID for ad targeting and frequency capping.

Your media files are never shared with AdMob.

You can reset or opt out of personalised advertising in Android Settings - Google - Ads. Buying Pro removes all ads on this device.

6. Google Play Purchases

All purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. FlipX Studio never sees your card number, billing address, or Google account email.

The only data we receive about a purchase is the product id and the purchase token used for server-side validation.

7. App Permissions

Media Access

Required to read the videos, audio, and images you pick for conversion.

Notifications

Used to surface job completion or failure.

Internet

Used for purchase validation, Google Fonts caching, and AdMob (Free tier only).

8. Children's Privacy

FlipX Studio is intended for general media-conversion users and is not directed to children under 13.

9. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and version at the top of this page.
