Privacy Policy

HSX StudioFlow - How your data is handled and protected.

Effective date: 1 Jun 2026  ·  Version: 1.0.0.0  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

HSX StudioFlow is a Windows desktop app for local AI image generation - text-to-image and image-to-image with SDXL and SD 1.5 checkpoints, plus face swap, face restore, face/hand detailing and upscaling.

All image generation and processing runs locally on your own computer and GPU. Your prompts, source images and generated images never leave your device and are never transmitted to Hasnain Studio X.

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

HSX StudioFlow does not collect or send the following to the developer:

Identity Data

No name, email, phone number, or personal identity data.

Your Images & Prompts

Prompts, source/reference faces, init images and generated images are processed locally and are never uploaded to our servers.

Telemetry

No analytics, usage tracking, ad tracking, or third-party telemetry SDKs are used.

Sensitive System Data

No passwords, credentials, keychain, or hidden account data is collected.

3. Data Stored Locally

Your Library

Models, LoRAs, upscalers, face models, presets and your generated images are stored locally in a folder you choose (your "library").

Settings & History

App settings, theme choice and recent-prompt history are stored locally and can be cleared from within the app.

Trial/License State

Trial and purchase status are managed by the Microsoft Store; the app caches only the resulting licence state locally.

4. Network & Third-Party Services

HSX StudioFlow runs offline for generation. It connects to the internet only in these cases, and never sends your images or prompts:

Model Downloads (you initiate)

The in-app Smart Manager can download AI models and components (for example from Hugging Face or GitHub) only when you choose to download them. These requests fetch public model files and send no personal data, account information, prompts or images.

Microsoft Store

Licence, trial and purchase checks are made through the Microsoft Store APIs on your device. No payment data is handled by the app.

No Generation Servers

There is no Hasnain Studio X cloud or inference server. Nothing you create is uploaded for processing.

5. Microsoft Store Purchase & Trial

HSX StudioFlow is a paid app with a free trial offered through the Microsoft Store. Payment, billing, trial length and refunds are handled entirely by Microsoft under Microsoft Store policies.

HSX StudioFlow does not process or store payment card details.

6. App Permissions

The app requests only internet access (for optional model downloads and Microsoft Store licence checks) and the standard full-trust desktop capability required to run its local AI engine on your GPU. System and hardware information is read locally to select your GPU and is never transmitted.

7. Face Data & Responsible Use

Face swapping and AI image generation are powerful - use them responsibly.

Processed Locally

Faces and images are detected, swapped, restored and generated entirely on your device. No face data, source image or result is uploaded to us or any third party.

Use Only Content You Own

You must only use images and faces that you own or have explicit permission to use. You are solely responsible for the content you process.

AI Watermark

Every generated or edited image is marked with a small "AI" watermark for transparency that it was created or altered with AI.

No Hidden Collection

There is no hidden data collection. The app does not gather, profile, or transmit your media or identity.

Prohibited uses. You must not use HSX StudioFlow for: non-consensual face swapping; deepfake impersonation for fraud or deception; adult or NSFW content; harmful political impersonation; or any misleading identity manipulation. Such use is strictly prohibited and is your sole legal responsibility.

8. Children's Privacy

HSX StudioFlow is a general-audience creative tool and is not directed to children under 13.

9. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and version on this page.
