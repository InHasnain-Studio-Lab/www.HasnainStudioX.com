Privacy Policy

PC TurboX Ultra - How your data is handled and protected.

Effective date: 19 June 2026  ·  Version: 1.0.0  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar

1. Overview

PC TurboX Ultra is a Windows performance and optimisation app. It cleans junk files, frees memory, manages startup items and power plans, finds large and duplicate files, uninstalls programs, and applies one-click Turbo Boost and Game Mode tuning - all using real Windows system data.

PC TurboX Ultra performs all optimisation work entirely on your PC. The contents of your files are never read, copied, recorded, or transmitted anywhere outside your machine.

We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

PC TurboX Ultra does not collect or send the following to the developer:

Identity Data

No name, email, phone number, account, or sign-in is ever requested or stored.

Your Files

File scans run locally to measure sizes and find duplicates. File contents are never uploaded; only sizes, paths, and hashes are used on your machine.

Usage Patterns

The app does not track which tools you use, how long you use them, or what actions you run.

Telemetry

No analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, no third-party trackers of any kind are included.

3. Data Stored Locally

App Settings

Your theme, accent, Turbo Boost and Game Mode preferences, and tool options are saved locally so they are restored on every launch.

Activity Log

A local log records optimisation actions you run (such as junk cleaned or memory freed), so you can review what the app changed. It stays on your PC.

Restore Points

Before system changes, the app can ask Windows to create a System Restore point. This is handled by Windows; PC TurboX Ultra stores no separate backup.

Licence State

A cached licence flag lets the app recognise your purchase offline, without contacting the Microsoft Store on every launch.

All local data is stored under %LOCALAPPDATA%\Packages\HasnainButtAkhtar.PCTurboXUltra\. Uninstalling the app removes all of this data.

4. Network Activity

Microsoft Store Licence Check

On first launch and when you tap Restore Purchase, PC TurboX Ultra contacts the Microsoft Store API to verify your licence. Only your Store account token is used - the app never sees your payment details.

Internet Speed Test

When you start a speed test, the app downloads a sample file from Cloudflare's public speed endpoint (speed.cloudflare.com) and measures latency with a standard network ping. No personal data is sent; the request is anonymous and runs only when you trigger it.

Outbound Links

Tapping hasnainstudiox.com or the privacy-policy link opens your default browser. No data is sent by the app itself in this action.

Beyond licence verification and the speed test you start yourself, PC TurboX Ultra makes no outbound network requests.

5. Microsoft Store Purchases

All purchases and the free trial are managed entirely by the Microsoft Store. PC TurboX Ultra never sees your payment card, billing address, or Microsoft account credentials.

The app receives only a licence object confirming whether your account holds an active purchase or an active trial, and the trial expiry date if applicable. No personally identifiable information is returned by this API call.

6. App Permissions

Internet Client

Required for Microsoft Store licence verification, the optional internet speed test, and to open web links in your browser.

Full Trust

Required to read real system metrics and perform optimisation tasks such as clearing temporary files, freeing memory, and managing startup items and power plans. Actions that need Windows administrator rights ask for your permission first.

7. Children's Privacy

PC TurboX Ultra is a general-purpose system utility and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Policy Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date and version number at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
