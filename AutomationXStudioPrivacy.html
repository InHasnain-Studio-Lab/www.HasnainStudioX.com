Privacy Policy

AutomationX Studio is developed by Hasnain Studio X with a local‑first, privacy‑first design philosophy.

Effective date: 16 July 2026  ·  Publisher: Hasnain Butt Akhtar / Hasnain Studio X  ·  Applies to: AutomationX Studio (Windows App)

1. Overview

AutomationX Studio is an offline automation studio developed by Hasnain Studio X with a local-first, privacy-first design philosophy.

  • We do not collect personal data.
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers.
  • We do not operate servers for our apps.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize user data.

Your flows run on your own device. Any network step is one you added yourself and points only where you tell it to.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

AutomationX Studio does not collect, store, or send any of the following:

Personal Identity Data

No name, email, phone number, address, or identifiers.

Usage Data or Analytics

No telemetry, crash analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Your Flows or Content

No flows, scripts, variables, exported bots, or any content you create inside the app.

Sensitive System Data

No passwords, tokens, credentials, browsing history, or system logs.

Location Data

No GPS, IP-based location, or device location.

We do not run servers, so nothing can be uploaded to us even by accident.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

App Settings

Your theme, accent, and run preferences are saved locally on your device only.

Your Flows and Data Hub

Flows, schedules, and the local Data Hub key-value store stay on your device and are never transmitted.

Local Cache

Temporary run logs and cache stored locally and never transmitted.

Local Trial or Licence State

Managed entirely by the Microsoft Store. The app reads it locally; we receive nothing.

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it as a pack or bot.

4. Microsoft Store Licensing

AutomationX Studio is a paid app with a free Microsoft Store trial. All licensing, payments, and transactions are handled exclusively by Microsoft.

  • We do not process payment information.
  • We do not store payment details.
  • We do not receive your Microsoft account information.

5. App Permissions

Minimum Required Only

AutomationX Studio requests only the minimum permissions required for core functionality, and uses them solely for app features, never for data collection.

File Access

Only the files and folders your flows target - never background scanning.

On-Device Processing

Every automation block runs locally on your PC using the Windows runtime bundled with the app.

Microphone (Aximo)

Used only while you are actively talking to Aximo, the voice assistant. Speech is recognised entirely on your device by Windows; no audio is recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. Aximo is optional - the microphone is never used unless you open it and press listen.

Network Access

Used only by network blocks you add (ping, HTTP request, download) and the Microsoft Store licence check. No data is sent to us.

6. Children's Privacy

AutomationX Studio is a general-purpose productivity utility. It is not directed to children under 13 and does not collect any data from any user.

7. Policy Updates

If this policy changes, we will update the version and effective date. Any changes will remain consistent with our core principle: Your data stays on your device.
